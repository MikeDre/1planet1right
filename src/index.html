Let's make it a human right
to live on a healthy planet

Campaign overview

For nature. For people. Let’s demand that the UN make it a universal human right to live on a healthy planet.

It’s no secret: our natural world is in terrible shape. The destruction of nature is causing climate chaos, driving millions of species to the brink of extinction and harms people directly, as COVID19 urgently reminds us. Lest we forget, we are part of nature, and we need a healthy planet to survive together sustainably. A post-COVID recovery must be a green recovery with the human right to a healthy natural environment at its core.

We need to completely change the way we treat our home. We, and all other living beings, deserve the right to a healthy natural world.

Join us: sign the petition to make it a UN recognized human right to live on a healthy planet. To emerge from these crises, to ensure our future and that of the planet, we need to entirely transform humanity’s relationship with nature. This human right helps make that happen.
